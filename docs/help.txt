Firstly, add some words to wordlist. Just say me some English words
After adding at least 8 words, you may start training with /train and end with /end
While training you will be proposed with one English word and four Russian translates and you need to choose the correct one
To remove word from word list use /rm index
To view list of all words use /list
Built with Yandex.Translate api.
